About Me


My name is Warren Spencer and I am a Software Engineer from Birmingham, AL. All of my life I have always been fascinated by tech. I have always found computers and electronics to be amazing devices and tools. That is why I decided to study Computer Science to learn about computers and software engineering. In 2020, I graduated from Lawson State Community College with an Associates in the Applied Science degree in Computer Science. In 2023, I graduated from the University of Alabama at Birmingham with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Computer Science. Software development is my passion! I am dedicated to developing software and applications. I am dedicated to staying relevant as a developer. I am also interested in AI, particularly Generative AI. My hobbies outside of software development are video games, LeetCode, and drawing. If you are interested in collaborating with me feel free to reach out!
